Compliance




Patient Panel Synthesizer is compliant with NISTIR 8053 - De-Identification of Personal Information. All identifying information, as per NISTIR 8053, has been removed and replaced with synthetic information.
Patient names (first and last) produced by Patient Panel Synthesizer are 100% synthetic. Any similarity to actual people with similar names is purely coincidental. Any similarily to real people of the synthetic patient records produced by Patient Panel Synthesizer is purely coincidental.
Patient addresses produced by Patient Panel Synthesizer are real addresses. There is no association nor relation between any patient record produced by Patient Panel Synthesizer and any real person associated with a address produced by Patient Panel Synthesizer. Any similarily to real people associated with an address synthesized by Patient Panel Synthesizer is purely coincidental.
